How It Works — Contest Rules

  • Each contest seats 20 players — top 3 of 20 finishers win prizes
  • 1st place wins 40% of the pool, 2nd wins 25%, 3rd wins 18%
  • Each contest lasts 90 seconds — 12 questions, 4 choices each
  • 5 points per correct answer, 0 for incorrect
  • Fastest correct answer in the field earns a +1 point speed bonus
  • An 18-second commercial break airs between Questions 6 and 7
  • Contest starts only when all 20 seats are filled
  • If fewer than 90% of seats fill, contest cancels — full refund issued
  • Cancel up to 1 hour before start for a full in-app refund
  • Questions drawn randomly across Black History categories
  • Winners must verify identity before prize funds are released
  • All players must be confirmed in advance — admin approval required
  • Players must be 18 years of age or older to participate
  • No AI assistance permitted: players may not use AI tools, external help, or assistance of any kind when answering questions. Solo play only.
Daily Schedule: The game day begins at 12:00 AM (0000 / midnight) and contests run in 90-second increments around the clock — 864 total contest slots per day, each seating 20 players (17,280 players/day max).
